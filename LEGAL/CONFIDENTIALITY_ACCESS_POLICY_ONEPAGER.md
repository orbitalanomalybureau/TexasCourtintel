## Purpose
Protect company source code, data, and methods as confidential trade secrets.

## Scope
Applies to all staff, contractors, vendors, and systems handling Company information.

## Data Classification
- **Public**: approved for external release
- **Internal**: non-public operational information
- **Confidential / Trade Secret**: source code, architectures, prompts/workflows, customer data, pricing strategy, analytics methods

## Core Rules
1. Need-to-know access only.
2. MFA required for code hosting, cloud, and admin tools.
3. No plaintext credentials in repositories or shared docs.
4. No external sharing of confidential materials without written approval.
5. Use approved storage/backup paths only.
6. Log and retain access and change history.

## Access Lifecycle
- **Provisioning**: manager approval + least privilege
- **Change**: ticketed, documented, reviewed
- **Revocation**: same-day at role change/termination

## Vendor/Contractor Requirements
- Signed NDA + IP assignment before access
- Access scoped to specific tasks
- Immediate revocation at project completion

## Incident Handling
If suspected leak/unauthorized access occurs:
1. Notify founder/admin immediately
2. Revoke credentials/tokens
3. Preserve logs and evidence
4. Notify counsel if material risk exists
5. Document corrective actions

## Enforcement
Policy violations may result in access removal, termination, legal action, and claims for injunctive relief.

## Review Cycle
Review and re-acknowledge quarterly or after material architecture/product changes.

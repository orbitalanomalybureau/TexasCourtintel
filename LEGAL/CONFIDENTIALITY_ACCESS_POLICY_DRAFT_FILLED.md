## Purpose
Protect Texas Court Intel LLC source code, data, and methods as confidential trade secrets.

## Scope
Applies to all staff, contractors, vendors, and systems handling Company information.

## Data Classification
- **Public**: approved website content and public-facing materials
- **Internal**: non-public operations and workflows
- **Confidential / Trade Secret**: source code, architecture, premium logic, prompts/workflows, customer usage data, pricing strategy, analytics methods

## Core Rules
1. Need-to-know access only.
2. MFA required for code hosting, cloud, and admin tools.
3. No plaintext credentials in repositories, chat logs, or shared docs.
4. No public disclosure of confidential details without written approval.
5. Use approved storage/backup paths only.
6. Maintain access/change logs and periodic reviews.
7. Only official-source judicial/court factual updates are permitted in production datasets.

## Access Lifecycle
- **Provisioning**: founder/admin approval + least privilege
- **Change**: documented and reviewable
- **Revocation**: same-day on role change/termination

## Current Operational Controls (as of 2026-02-24)
- Private project repository with tracked commits
- External backup mirror to ArmorATD drive on schedule
- Handoff documentation maintained for continuity
- Premium legal-intelligence access model tied to attorney verification direction

## Vendor/Contractor Requirements
- Signed NDA + IP assignment before access
- Access limited to assigned tasks
- Immediate access removal when engagement ends

## Incident Handling
If suspected leak/unauthorized access occurs:
1. Notify founder/admin immediately
2. Revoke affected credentials/tokens
3. Preserve logs and evidence
4. Notify counsel for material exposure
5. Document corrective actions and prevention plan

## Enforcement
Policy violations may result in access removal, termination, legal action, and claims for injunctive relief.

## Review Cycle
- Monthly control review
- Quarterly formal policy re-acknowledgment
- Immediate update after major platform/security changes
